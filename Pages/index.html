WHO ARE WE?

ECELL: Entrepreneurship Cell - Dubai is established at BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus. It is an environment that fosters entrepreneurship and nurtures the willingness to develop, organise, and manage a business venture among students. It provides opportunities for young minds to develop ideas into successful ventures by understanding entrepreneurship, analyzing product needs, and creating economic impact.

The center aims to instill the spirit of an entrepreneurial mindset in students. It offers networking events and speaker series on business, management, marketing, and entrepreneurship. ECELL plans to inspire students through presentations by outstanding business leaders and provide networking opportunities via workshops, seminars, and competitions.
